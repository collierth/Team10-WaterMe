About Water Me

This app was developed by six Georgia State University student who decided that there should be a better way of managing the watering of specific plant species, also to avoid the over watering of plants.

There are many species of plants and some plants can't be watered to many times in one week.

Steps on how to water your plant

  1. Provide water based on the native environment of the plant.
  2. Soak the area around a plant after you put it in the ground. .
  3. Test the moisture with your finger.
  4. Use drip irrigation for plants with shallow roots.
